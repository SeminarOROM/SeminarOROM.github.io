Purpose & Registration Guidelines

This seminar will invite researchers from academia or companies, with a focus on featuring talks that present cutting-edge research at the intersection of Operations Research (OR), Operations Management (OM), Computer Science (CS), and Mathematics. The goal is to enhance communication across diverse research domains and industries, fostering the initiation of new research projects and facilitating the expansion of your professional network.

If you want to register this seminar, kindly send a request to this sheet (Very simple and free).
Upon submitting your request, you will be added to our email list. Subsequently, when we have a new presenter, you will receive both notification and the Zoom link for participation.
